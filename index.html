designer, artist, programmer; computer science, human-computer interaction design, fine art.

I'm a recent graduate from the University of British Columbia, in Computer Science and Visual Art.

I'm currently the web and design assistant for the Art History, Visaul Art and Theory Department at UBC – keeping their websites up to date, generating new content, and doing any in-house design work (posters, information graphics, etc.).
