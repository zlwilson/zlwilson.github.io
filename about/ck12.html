Ck-12 Foundation

Related Skills

Illustration, Graphic Design, Interaction Research, Usability Testing

I began working at the Ck-12 Foundation as a design intern after highschool, then continued there as a designer and illustrator when their in-house designer left.

Sketching interactions to communicate new ideas for their web e-book builder.

I began developing website and app prototypes for the executive team, then started illustrating their textbooks, especially those in science and math. With one other design intern, I created the concept and template for a new "Study Guide" product they wanted to offer.

"Ck-12 Guides" template.

Later they introduced a new tool for science teachers to build their own interactive science questions ("PLIXes"), and a library of pre-built questions. I helped to populate their new library, and in the process helped to refine and debug these tools, and investigate what functions were necessary, were missing, or weren't needed.

An early version of the "PLIX" tool I helped to conduct usability testing on.
