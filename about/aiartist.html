The AI Artist

Related Skills

Machine Learning, Digital Fabrication, Rapid Prototyping

This project was an interactive, mixed-media sculpture to illustrate the potential reality of a future AI artist. With 2 other designers and engineers, we trained a GAN to generate original paintings, and visualized the neural network with a sculpture to display the paintings.

The final sculpture displayed at the GDP office in Orsay, France.

A sequence of portrait paintings generated by our GAN.

The final artwork could be interacted with through a QR code that connected visitors' phones to the artwork.

An early prototype of the sculpture using cardboard.

Laser etched layers of plastic help to visualize the neural network.
