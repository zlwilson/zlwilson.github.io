Art Practice
2014 - 2019

In addition to the residency and work with Melt Collective, I worked on many other art projects during my degree and time in Vancouver.
These varied from more traditional paintings, to working with analog and digital printing processes, to photography and digital artwork, to experiments with Arduinos and wearable technology, to robotic arms, and some assorted illustration projects for indie films.
