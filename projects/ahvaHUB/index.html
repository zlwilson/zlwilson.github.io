Graphics/Media Assistant
Visual Art Department (UBC)
Sept 2016 - Aug 2018

Vancouver, BC

I worked for the department for two years as a designer. At the beginning I created designs for visiting artists and exhibitions.
Then I was given charge of the website, to update content and preserve the look-and-feel across their entire web presence (link).

In my last six months there I was given a brief to redesign their website to more directly address the needs of both the staff and the students.
I interviewed the various lab technicians, students, and administrators in charge of trainings.
Based on this information I restructured the website into an information hub targeted towards students, and restructured the inventory and classification systems to ease information input for technicians and administrators (link).
I also implemented a new QR code system for the art facilities to ease access to sitauted information in various labs.
