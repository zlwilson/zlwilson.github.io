Human-Centered
Design Work
2016 - 2019

Vancouver, BC - Enschede, NL

Throughout my studies I have worked on many different design projects to practice user-centered design principles,
rapid prototyping techniques, iterative design, and more.

At the Universtiy of British Columbia I worked on a prototype system to integrate workflow in smart kitchens
with a home grocery delivery systems offered by SaveOn Foods, we developed low fidelity prototypes and ran a user study.
I also worked in a team to design and run a study that explored how people use different social media and messaging platforms to organize community events.
This project concluded with a set of design guidelines to support local community organizing, and a medium fidelity prototype.

During my masters studies in The Netherlands and France I have worked on many design projects, such as TerraTree, WorkeR, Tippie, and a redesign of the Couchsurfing platform using the emotion capture card technique.
