Tippie
Summer, 2019

Budapest, HU

In a team of 6, we developed and prototyped a cashless payment solution for street performers and other community art events.
We interviewed local musicians and artists, and iterated on the design of a cashless payment solution for these artists. We developed a business plan and created a prototype of the software platform as well as the physical interface of the card reader we developed.
We presented our work to the local team at Novopayments and a panel of local entrepreneurs.
