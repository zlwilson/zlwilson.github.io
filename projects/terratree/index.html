TerraTree
Spring, 2019

Enschede, NL

In a team of 5, we developed and created a multi-modal, interactive art installation for a historic city site in Enschede, NL.
Through many cycles of ideation and rapid prototyping, and working in cooperation with the city and local historical society, we developed an installation to help visitors to a new park discover stories about the historic city.

I am currently developing this project into a research paper in collaboration with two professors at the University of Twente.
We are exploring the design and prototyping process we used to design for this specific place,
and how that can be generalized into design and design-teaching guidelines.
