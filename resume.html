Experience
  • Samsung + eDAPT Lab (UBC) | Vancouver, BC - 2017-18

    Undergraduate Researcher

    Working with one other undergraduate researcher, I built a prototype kiosk to help seniors learn to use smart phones, which expanded on a proptype developed by a reasearcher at the Samsung UX research lab in Vancouver, BC. We began with lightweight user testing to gather initial feedback while we iterated on the design of the kiosk, and finally we designed and ran a controlled experiment in collaboration with a post-doc in Japan which was presented as a poster at CSCW '18.

  • Melt Collective | Vancouver, BC - 2018

    Resident Artist

    For 6 months I worked on art projects in the Melt Collective lab at UBC. I helped ideate then prototyped creative uses for recycled plastics. I developed a technique to mold plastic from waste plastic bags, then I built a prototype archway by manufacturing bricks using this technique and plastic bags gathered from the city.

  • Visual Art Department (UBC) | Vancouver, BC - 2016-18

    Graphics/Media Production Assistant

    Based on data I gathered from the varied labs of the art department, I reoprganized the website of the department into a new online training and information hub for students to access all relevant safety, training and other information. I also streamlined some backend processes for the administration by simplifing information input, and reorganized data to enable more automation. As the departments designer I also created promotional material for visiting artist events and exhibition openings, kept their website up to date.

  • Ck-12 | Palo Alto, CA - 2011-16

  • 4Band | Mountain View, CA - 2013-14
